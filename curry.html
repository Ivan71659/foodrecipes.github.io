Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast (or use store-bought chicken tenders)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 egg
  • Half cup breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying
  • Curry sauce:
    • 1 cup water
    • 1 potato (peeled and chopped)
    • 1 carrot (chopped)
    • Half onion (chopped)
    • 2 blocks of japanese curry roux

Steps

  1. Cooking the curry:
    • In a small pot, boil chopped onion, carrot, and potato in 1 cup of water until soft (10‚Äì15 min).
    • Add curry roux cubes and stir until sauce thickens.
  2. Make chicken katsu:
    • Slice chicken breast in half to make thinner pieces
    • Season with salt and pepper
    • Dip in beaten egg, then coat in breadcrumbs.
    • Pan-fry in oil over medium heat until golden and cooked through (about 3-4 min per side)
    • Slice into strips
  3. Assemble:
    • Put rice on a plate.
    • Pour curry over rice.
    • Top with sliced chicken
