Pad Thai

Ingredients

  • 1 pack rice noodles
  • 1 egg
  • half cup cook chicken, shrimp, or tofu
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 glove garlic
  • half cup bean sprout
  • 1 green onion
  • crush peanut and lime wedge for topping

Steps

  1. Soak or cook noodles according to package.
  2. Mix sauce: soy sauce, peanut butter, sugar, and a splash of water.
  3. Stir-fry: Heat oil, add garlic, then protein. Push aside, scramble egg in pan.
  4. Add noodles and sauce. Stir everything until combined.
  5. Top with peanuts, green onion, and a squeeze of lime.
